Who are we

About Us

Earth's Child Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating a healthier, more sustainable world by prioritizing the well-being of both the environment and its people. We address environmental challenges and enhance quality of life through targeted solutions, collaboration, and strategic partnerships.

With a vision to become Africa's most trusted and impactful non-profit, we work with key sectors to influence environmental and economic decision-makers. Our mission is to drive positive, lasting change by implementing initiatives that improve environmental health and promote well-being in Nigerian and African communities. Through collective efforts, we inspire stewardship, responsibility, and interconnectedness for a better future.

What we do

Area of course

Environmental Conservation

We combat deforestation through reforestation and tree-planting initiatives, improving air quality and preserving biodiversity. Also, we advocate for sustainable land use

Community Health & Well-Being

We provide access to clean water, sanitation, and health education for sustainable living. Our initiatives support community-based healthcare programs to enhance overall well-being.

Education and Awarness

We run outreach programs to raise awareness about environmental issues and sustainable practices. Scholarships and educational support empower underprivileged communities to thrive.

Climatic Action

We mitigate climate change through renewable energy projects and responsible consumption advocacy. Our initiatives encourage sustainable solutions for a resilient future.
